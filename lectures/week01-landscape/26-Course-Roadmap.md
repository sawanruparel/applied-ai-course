# Course Roadmap: 15 Weeks

## Weeks 1-3: Foundations

- Week 1: The 2026 AI Landscape (today)
- Week 2: Prompt Engineering & Structured Outputs
- Week 3: APIs, SDKs, and Model Integration

## Weeks 4-6: Core Patterns

- Week 4: Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Week 5: Function Calling & Tool Use
- Week 6: Agents and Autonomous Systems

## Weeks 7-9: Building Systems

- Week 7: Evaluation and Testing
- Week 8: Compound AI Systems & Orchestration
- Week 9: Fine-Tuning and Adaptation

## Weeks 10-12: Production & Safety

- Week 10: Deployment, Scaling, and Cost Optimization
- Week 11: Multimodal Applications
- Week 12: Safety, Red-Teaming, and Responsible AI

## Weeks 13-15: Capstone

- Week 13: Advanced Topics (inference-time compute, open models)
- Week 14: Capstone Project Work
- Week 15: Capstone Presentations
