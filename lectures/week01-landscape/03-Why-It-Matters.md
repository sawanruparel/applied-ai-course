# Why This Matters for Practitioners

- **Model selection is now multi-dimensional** -- cost, latency, reasoning depth, and context length all trade off against each other
- **Prompt engineering is evolving** -- reasoning models need less hand-holding but benefit from structured problem decomposition
- **Architecture choices changed** -- compound AI systems with multiple models beat a single monolithic call
- **Open-weight models are competitive** -- DeepSeek R1, Llama 3, Qwen 2.5 mean you can self-host reasoning
- **Evaluation is harder** -- benchmarks are saturating; you need domain-specific evals
- **Cost profiles are shifting** -- a 30-second o3 call costs more than 100 GPT-4o calls, but may be worth it

> The skill gap is no longer "can you call an API?" -- it is "can you design systems that use these capabilities effectively?"
