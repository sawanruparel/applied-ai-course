# Week 14 Recap

## Safety & Red-Teaming

**Prompt injection** is the #1 vulnerability -- defend with multiple layers, not a single filter.

**Guardrails** (LlamaGuard, NeMo, Guardrails AI) provide automated input/output safety enforcement.

**Red-teaming** is continuous -- use human creativity and automated tools to find vulnerabilities before attackers do.

**Agent hijacking** is the highest-stakes threat -- restrict tool access, validate every action, apply least privilege.
