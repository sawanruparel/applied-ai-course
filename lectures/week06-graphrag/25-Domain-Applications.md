## Financial Services
A $330B investment firm uses GraphRAG to connect deal records, market trends, and regulatory filings. Queries traverse entity networks to surface compliance risks and investment patterns that vector search misses entirely.

## Healthcare
MedGraphRAG organizes patient records, treatment histories, drug interactions, and medical literature hierarchically. One implementation achieved 98.2% accuracy connecting patient data with clinical knowledge.

## Legal & Compliance
Precedent networks link cases, statutes, and regulations through citation graphs. GraphRAG enables queries like "What cases support this legal argument?" by traversing citation chains, not just matching keywords.

## Manufacturing
Multi-modal GraphRAG connects equipment maintenance records with part specifications and supplier relationships. Enables supply chain risk queries that span multiple document types and time periods.
