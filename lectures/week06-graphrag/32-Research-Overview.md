# GraphRAG Research Landscape (2024-2026)

- Rapid growth: from one foundational paper (April 2024) to **3 major surveys** and dozens of methods papers by early 2026
- Key venues: arXiv, NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, KDD, ACM TOIS
- Research is converging on three axes: **reducing cost**, **improving graph quality**, and **standardizing evaluation**

| Survey | Focus | So What? |
|--------|-------|----------|
| [Graph RAG: A Survey](https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.08921) (ACM TOIS, 2025) | Formalizes the workflow: indexing, retrieval, generation | The field's taxonomy — use it to understand where any new paper fits |
| [Survey of GraphRAG for Customized LLMs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.13958) (arXiv, Jan 2025) | Technical foundations across professional domains | Broadest coverage of domain-specific applications |
| [RAG with Graphs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.00309) (arXiv, Jan 2025) | Comprehensive analysis of graph-augmented retrieval | Good starting point for understanding the full landscape |

> The research is moving faster than the tooling — read the papers to stay ahead of the frameworks.
