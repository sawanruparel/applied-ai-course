# The Knowledge Problem

LLMs are impressive generalists -- but they don't know **your** data.

## What LLMs Know

- General world knowledge up to training cutoff
- Common programming patterns, APIs, and frameworks
- Published scientific literature, textbooks, Wikipedia
- General reasoning and language understanding
- Public information available at scale on the internet

## What You Actually Need

- Your company's internal documentation and policies
- Private codebases, architecture decisions, runbooks
- Customer data, support tickets, CRM records
- Real-time data: stock prices, sensor feeds, logs
- Domain-specific knowledge not well-represented in training data
- Information created **after** the model's training cutoff

> The gap between general knowledge and actionable, domain-specific knowledge is the central problem of applied AI.
