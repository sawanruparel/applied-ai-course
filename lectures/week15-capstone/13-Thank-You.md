# Thank You

## What I hope you take away

A working mental model of how to **build, evaluate, and operate** AI-powered systems — not a tour of frameworks or a survey of papers.

The field will keep moving. The model choices, framework choices, and "best practices" we covered in May 2026 will be outdated by next year. The reasoning behind them — how to decide, what to measure, when to escalate — will not.

## If you only remember three things

1. **Measure before you optimize.** No metric = no progress
2. **The host owns the trust boundary.** Don't outsource what you can't audit
3. **Cheap and well-evaluated beats expensive and unevaluated**, every time
