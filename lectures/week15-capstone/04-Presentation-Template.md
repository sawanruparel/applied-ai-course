# Presentation Template (12 Minutes Per Team)

## 1. Problem (2 min)
Business context, current pain point, why existing solutions fall short — make the audience feel the problem

## 2. Solution (2 min)
Your approach, key insight, why generative AI is the right tool — the "aha moment"

## 3. Architecture (2 min)
System diagram, model choices, MCP server integrations, data flow — how the pieces fit together

## 4. Live Demo (3 min)
Walk through a real scenario end-to-end — show the system thinking, acting, and recovering from errors

## 5. Results (2 min)
Evaluation metrics, baseline comparisons, cost analysis — hard numbers that prove it works

## 6. Learnings (1 min)
What surprised you, what broke, what you would change — honest reflection

**+ 3 minutes Q&A** | Total: 15 minutes per team
