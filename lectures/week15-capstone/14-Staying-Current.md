# Staying Current in Applied AI

### Conferences
- **NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR** — top ML research venues; watch for applied AI workshops
- **[AI Engineer Summit](https://www.ai.engineer/)** — practitioner-focused; system design and deployment patterns
- **Strange Loop / QCon** — software engineering meets AI at scale

### Blogs and Newsletters
- [Anthropic Research Blog](https://www.anthropic.com/research), [OpenAI Blog](https://openai.com/blog), [Google DeepMind Blog](https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/) — model provider insights
- [The Batch](https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/) (Andrew Ng) — weekly AI news digest
- [Simon Willison's Weblog](https://simonwillison.net/) — hands-on LLM tooling and experimentation
- [Latent Space Podcast](https://www.latent.space/podcast) — deep dives with AI engineering practitioners

### Papers to Track
- arXiv cs.CL and cs.AI — filter by citation count and author reputation
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) alerts — set up keyword notifications for your interests
- [Papers With Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) — find implementations alongside research

### Communities
- [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/) community and model hubs
- [MCP specification GitHub](https://github.com/modelcontextprotocol/modelcontextprotocol) and community forums
- Local AI meetups and university research groups

---

**The field moves fast. Build a habit of reading one paper or post per week — it compounds.**
