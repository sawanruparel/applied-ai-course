# Lessons Learned

### What Worked
- Which design decisions paid off? Why?
- What course concepts were most directly useful in your build?
- Any surprising wins — things that worked better than expected?

### What Didn't Work
- What did you try that failed? (This is the most valuable part)
- Prompt strategies that seemed right but broke in practice
- Architecture decisions you had to reverse mid-project

### What You'd Do Differently
- If you started over with what you know now, what changes?
- Would you pick different models? Different MCP server design?
- Where would you invest more time: data quality, eval, UX?

### Advice for Future Teams
- What do you wish someone had told you at the start?
- Key debugging techniques you discovered
- Resources that were unexpectedly helpful

---

**Honest reflection demonstrates mastery. Knowing what went wrong shows you understand the space deeply.**
