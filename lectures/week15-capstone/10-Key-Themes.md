# Key Themes Across the Course

## Reasoning
From System 1/System 2 thinking (Week 1) to chain-of-thought prompting (Week 2) to multi-step agentic planning (Week 10) — the core challenge is getting models to think well.

## Grounding
Embeddings and vector search (Week 4), RAG pipelines (Week 5), knowledge graphs — models are only as good as the context they receive. Garbage in, hallucinations out.

## Action
MCP protocol (Week 9), tool use, multi-agent orchestration (Week 10) — the shift from models that talk to systems that do. This is where AI becomes operational.

## Efficiency
Small models (Week 12), distillation (Week 13), cost optimization — production AI must be fast and affordable. The best model is the cheapest one that meets your quality bar.

## Safety
Guardrails, red-teaming (Week 14), human-in-the-loop (Week 11) — every system that acts autonomously must have boundaries. Trust is earned through transparency.

---

These five themes are the pillars of applied AI engineering.
