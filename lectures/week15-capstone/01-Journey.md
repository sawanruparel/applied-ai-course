# The Journey: From Prompting to Autonomous Systems

## Phase 1: Foundations (Weeks 1-3)
AI landscape, advanced prompting, developer stack — learning to think in tokens and build with LLM APIs

## Phase 2: Knowledge Augmentation (Weeks 4-6)
Knowledge representation, RAG 2.0, embeddings — grounding models in real-world data

## Phase 3: The Agentic Shift (Weeks 7-9)
Evaluation, MCP protocol, building MCP servers — giving models the ability to reason and act

## Phase 4: Compound Systems (Weeks 10-11)
Multi-agent workflows, human-in-the-loop, memory — orchestrating systems that collaborate

## Phase 5: Efficiency & Governance (Weeks 12-14)
Small models, distillation, fine-tuning, safety, red-teaming — making it fast, cheap, and safe

---

Each phase built on the last. Your capstone ties them all together.
