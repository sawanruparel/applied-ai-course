# Framing the Problem

### Set the Business Context
- Who is the user? What is their role and daily workflow?
- What is the current process — and what makes it painful?
- Quantify the cost: time wasted, errors made, revenue lost

### Explain Why AI Helps Here
- What makes this problem suited to generative AI specifically?
- Is it language understanding? Reasoning over documents? Multi-step coordination?
- Why couldn't a traditional rule-based system solve this?

### Define the Scope
- What does your system do — and what does it explicitly NOT do?
- Where is the human in the loop? (Week 11: not every decision should be automated)
- What is the minimum viable version vs. the full vision?

### Common Pitfalls
- Too vague: "We're using AI to improve customer service"
- Too broad: trying to solve everything at once
- No baseline: if you can't measure the "before," you can't prove the "after"

---

**Strong problem framing is half the battle. Nail this and the rest follows.**
