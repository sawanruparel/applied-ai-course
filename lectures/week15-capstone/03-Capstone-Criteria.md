# What Makes a Great Capstone

### Real Problem
- Addresses a genuine business or domain pain point
- Not a toy demo — something a stakeholder would care about
- Clear articulation of who benefits and why it matters

### Working System
- End-to-end functional prototype (not just slides)
- MCP-powered architecture with tool integrations
- Demonstrates agentic reasoning: plan, act, observe, adapt

### Measurable Results
- Quantitative evaluation against a baseline (Week 7 eval frameworks)
- Honest reporting of failures and limitations
- Cost and latency analysis — could this run in production?

### Thoughtful Design
- Appropriate model selection — not everything needs GPT-4 (Week 12)
- Safety guardrails in place (Week 14)
- Human-in-the-loop where it matters (Week 11)

**Scoring: Problem clarity (20%) | Architecture (25%) | Demo (25%) | Evaluation (20%) | Learnings (10%)**
