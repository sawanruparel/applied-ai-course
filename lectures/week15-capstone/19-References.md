# Course-Wide Reference List

### Foundational Papers
- Vaswani et al. (2017). ["Attention Is All You Need."](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762) NeurIPS.
- Brown et al. (2020). ["Language Models are Few-Shot Learners."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.14165) NeurIPS.
- Wei et al. (2022). ["Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning in Large Language Models."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903) NeurIPS.
- Kaplan et al. (2020). ["Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2001.08361) arXiv.

### Retrieval and Knowledge
- Lewis et al. (2020). ["Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.11401) NeurIPS.
- Asai et al. (2023). ["Self-RAG: Learning to Retrieve, Generate, and Critique through Self-Reflection."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.11511) arXiv.
- Sarthi et al. (2024). ["RAPTOR: Recursive Abstractive Processing for Tree-Organized Retrieval."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.18059) ICLR.

### Agents and Tool Use
- Yao et al. (2023). ["ReAct: Synergizing Reasoning and Acting in Language Models."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03629) ICLR.
- Schick et al. (2023). ["Toolformer: Language Models Can Teach Themselves to Use Tools."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.04761) NeurIPS.
- Anthropic (2024). ["Model Context Protocol Specification."](https://modelcontextprotocol.io) GitHub.

### Efficiency and Distillation
- Hinton et al. (2015). ["Distilling the Knowledge in a Neural Network."](https://arxiv.org/abs/1503.02531) arXiv.
- Sanh et al. (2019). ["DistilBERT, a distilled version of BERT."](https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.01108) arXiv.
- Gou et al. (2021). ["Knowledge Distillation: A Survey."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.05525) IJCV.

### Safety and Alignment
- Bai et al. (2022). ["Constitutional AI: Harmlessness from AI Feedback."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.08073) arXiv.
- Perez et al. (2022). ["Red Teaming Language Models with Language Models."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2202.03286) arXiv.
- Anthropic (2023). ["Claude's Character."](https://www.anthropic.com/research/claude-character) Anthropic Research.

### Evaluation
- Zheng et al. (2023). ["Judging LLM-as-a-Judge with MT-Bench and Chatbot Arena."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.05685) NeurIPS.
- Chang et al. (2024). ["A Survey on Evaluation of Large Language Models."](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.03109) TMLR.
