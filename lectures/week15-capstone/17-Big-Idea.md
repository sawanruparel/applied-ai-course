# The Big Idea

## The best AI systems amplify human capability — they don't replace it.

---

Every great capstone project you will see today has a human at the center.

The agent retrieves, reasons, and acts — but the human defines the goal, judges the quality, and makes the final call.

This is not a limitation. It is the design.

---

**Human-in-the-loop is not a safety net. It is the architecture.**
