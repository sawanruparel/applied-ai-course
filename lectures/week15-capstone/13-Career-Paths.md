# Career Paths in Applied AI

## AI Engineer
Build and deploy LLM-powered applications. Design prompts, orchestrate agents, integrate MCP servers, and own the full stack from model selection to production monitoring. *Most directly aligned with this course.*

## ML Engineer
Focus on model training, fine-tuning, and optimization. Distillation pipelines (Week 13), evaluation infrastructure (Week 7), and model serving at scale. Deeper on the model side, less on the application side.

## Solutions Architect
Bridge business problems and technical solutions. Requires deep understanding of what AI can and cannot do, system design skills, and the ability to scope projects realistically. Week 5 (RAG) and Week 12 (small models) are your toolkit.

## AI Safety / Red Team Engineer
Specialize in adversarial testing, guardrail design, and compliance. Week 14 is your foundation. Growing demand as regulation increases and agentic systems gain autonomy.

---

All four roles need the same foundation: understanding models, building systems, evaluating results, and shipping safely.
