## Structure > Similarity
Vector RAG finds *similar* text. GraphRAG traverses *connected* knowledge. For multi-hop and global queries, structure wins decisively.

## Not a Replacement
GraphRAG complements vector RAG — don't replace, combine. Use vectors for breadth, graphs for depth. Hybrid architectures are the emerging default.

## Cost Is the Bottleneck
Full GraphRAG indexing is expensive. But LazyGraphRAG, LinearRAG, and hybrid approaches are collapsing this cost. The tradeoff space is evolving fast.

## Know Your Queries
GraphRAG's value scales with query complexity and entity density. Profile your actual query patterns before committing — simple queries don't benefit from graph structure.
