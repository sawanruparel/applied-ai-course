# Notable Papers

| Paper | Venue | Key Contribution |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| [Edge et al. — From Local to Global](https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.16130) | arXiv 2024 | Founded the field; community summaries for global search |
| [LazyGraphRAG](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/blog/lazygraphrag-setting-a-new-standard-for-quality-and-cost/) | Microsoft Research, 2024 | 0.1% indexing cost; deferred summarization |
| [HippoRAG](https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.14831) | NeurIPS 2024 | Memory-inspired retrieval via personalized PageRank |
| [HippoRAG2](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.14802) | ICML 2025 | Non-parametric continual learning for LLMs |
| [LinearRAG](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.10114) | ICLR 2026 | Relation-free graph construction; eliminates noisy edges |
| [KET-RAG](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.09304) | KDD 2025 | Multi-granular indexing for cost efficiency |
| [GraphRAG-Bench](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.05690) | ICLR 2026 | First standardized benchmark: when do graphs actually help? |
| [E2GraphRAG](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.24226) | arXiv 2025 | SpaCy + LLM hybrid for efficient extraction |
| [MedGraphRAG](https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.04187) | ACL 2025 | Hierarchical medical knowledge organization |
| [Practical GraphRAG at Scale](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.03226) | arXiv 2025 | Production deployment patterns and hybrid retrieval |

> **Reading order for builders:** Edge et al. → LazyGraphRAG → the ACM TOIS survey → whichever domain paper matches your use case.
